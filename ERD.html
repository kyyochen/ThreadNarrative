Enfants Riches Deprimes

Enfants Riches Déprimés, which translates to "Depressed Rich Kids," is a luxury fashion brand founded in 2012 by Henri Alexander Levy. Known for its punk-inspired aesthetic, the brand merges high fashion with the raw, rebellious energy of streetwear. Enfants Riches Déprimés is characterized by its limited-edition pieces, hand-distressed garments, and often controversial graphic designs that draw on themes of nihilism, existentialism, and the ennui of the elite. The brand's collections feature a mix of luxury materials and rough, DIY elements, including hand-painted leather jackets, distressed knitwear, and bold, statement-making accessories. Despite its relatively recent establishment, Enfants Riches Déprimés has quickly garnered a cult following among celebrities and fashion insiders, praised for its unapologetic attitude and unique blend of art and fashion.
